Our Love Story

How We Met

It was a rainy autumn evening in October 2019 at a cozy coffee shop in downtown Portland. Emma was reading her favorite novel when Lucas accidentally spilled his latte. What started as an embarrassing moment turned into hours of conversation and laughter.

The Proposal

Two years later, Lucas planned the perfect surprise. He took Emma back to that same coffee shop where they first met. After a nostalgic walk through their favorite spots, he got down on one knee in the park where they had their first picnic. With the sunset painting the sky in shades of pink and gold, Emma said yes through happy tears.

The dress code is Garden Formal / Cocktail Attire. Think elegant and sophisticated with a garden party vibe. Ladies: cocktail dresses, midi dresses, or elegant jumpsuits. Gentlemen: suits or dress shirts with slacks. Since we're outdoors for the ceremony, we recommend avoiding stiletto heels!

We love your little ones, but we've decided to make our wedding an adults-only celebration. We hope this gives you a chance to enjoy a night off and celebrate with us! Children in the wedding party are of course welcome.

Due to limited venue capacity, we're only able to accommodate those guests formally invited on your wedding invitation. If your invitation includes a plus-one, their name will be indicated. If you have questions, please reach out to us directly.

Yes! Both venues offer complimentary valet parking. We also encourage carpooling or using ride-share services if you plan to enjoy the open bar.

Portland in June is typically beautiful! Average temperatures range from 70–75°F (21–24°C). The ceremony will be outdoors, so we recommend bringing a light jacket for the evening.

Yes! We'll have a full open bar with wine, beer, cocktails, and non-alcoholic options. Signature cocktails include “The Blushing Bride” and “Lucky Lucas.”
